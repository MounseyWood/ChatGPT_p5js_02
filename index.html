3D Cloth Simulation

Touch/click on the cloth (in the canvas) to drag points (3D dragging not implemented). Adjust the controls below.

Pinning Mode:
Choose whether the cloth is pinned along the top edge or only at its corners.
Damping:
Controls energy loss (smooths motion).
Iterations:
Number of constraint relaxations per frame.
Stretch:
Affects how much the cloth can extend.
Shear:
Resists diagonal distortion.
Bending:
Controls resistance to sharp folds.
Weight:
Modifies the downward pull on the cloth.
Wind X:
Wind force along the X-axis.
Wind Y:
Wind force along the Y-axis.
Wind Z:
Wind force along the Z-axis.
Wind Buffer:
Scales overall wind oscillation amplitude.
Gravity:
Sets the base downward acceleration.

Simulation Attributes:
• Damping: Energy loss (smooths motion).
• Iterations: Constraint relaxations per frame.
• Stretch: Cloth extension capability.
• Shear: Resistance to diagonal distortion.
• Bending: Resistance to sharp folds.
• Weight & Gravity: Downward pull on the cloth.
• Wind (X, Y, Z): 3D wind vector.
• Wind Buffer: Scales wind oscillation amplitude.
• Shading Mode: Fills cells with a gray‑scale color based on vertical position.
• Pinning Mode: “Top” vs. “Corners.”
