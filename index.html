3D Cloth Simulation

Touch/click on the cloth (in the canvas) to drag points (not implemented in 3D). Adjust the sliders to change parameters.

Simulation Attributes:
• Damping: Controls energy loss (smooth motion).
• Iterations: Number of constraint relaxations per frame.
• Stretch / Shear / Bending: Distortion constraints.
• Weight / Gravity: Downward pull on the cloth.
• Wind (X,Y,Z): 3D wind vector.
• Wind Buffer: A single multiplier for wind amplitude. Higher = stronger oscillations.
• Shading Mode: When enabled, each cell is filled with a gray‑scale color based on its vertical position.
• Pinning Mode: “Top” or “Corners.”
