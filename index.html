3D Cloth Simulation

Touch/click on the cloth (in the canvas) to drag points. Adjust the sliders to change parameters.

Simulation Attributes:
• Damping: Controls energy loss (smooth motion).
• Iterations: Number of constraint relaxations per frame.
• Stretch: Affects how much the cloth can extend.
• Shear: Resists diagonal distortion.
• Bending: Controls resistance to sharp folds.
• Weight: Modifies the downward pull.
• Wind: Applied as a 3D vector (X, Y, Z).
• Gravity: Sets the base downward acceleration.
• Shading Mode: When enabled, fills each cell with a gray‑scale color based on its vertical position.
• Pinning Mode: Choose whether the cloth is pinned along the top edge or only at its four corners.
